Process

Four stations. You always know where your job is.

Each station names what happens, what you hold in your hand after, and how long it takes.

Station 1 · Listen — 15 minutes

The conversation. No pitch deck.

You talk through the business; I ask what actually needs to happen — bookings, sales, being found. You leave with an honest read whether or not we build.

You hold: a fixed written quote
Station 2 · Set the type — week one

Design, in your brand's voice.

A working preview on a private link — not wireframes, the real thing taking shape. You react, we adjust. Nothing goes to press without your nod.

You hold: a live preview link
Station 3 · Press — launch week

Built, wired, live.

Booking routed to you, Google Business Profile set, analytics on, every page tested on the phone in your pocket. Launch is a checklist, not a hope.

You hold: your site, live on your domain
Station 4 · Care — ongoing, optional

A real person, usually same-day.

Updates, seasonal refreshes, watchful eyes on performance and backups. Earned monthly — cancel anytime and the site is still yours.

You hold: a number that answers
